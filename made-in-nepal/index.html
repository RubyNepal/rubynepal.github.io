Made in Nepal

This page contains our projects as well as all other projects which are made in Nepal that use Ruby and related technologies.


Emitii

Emitii is an online time tracking tool and analysis tool designed for software development teams

Talk Ruby

Talk ruby is an online news aggregator about ruby and ruby discussion platform for ruby developers

CloudFactory

CloudFactory, a Kathmandu based startup is a Distributed Workforce and Enterprise Crowdsourcing Platform.

Mero Campus

an Online Marketplace for College Admissions in Nepal.

Ruby In tab

PhoneGap based android app that enables you to play ruby in your android devices.
